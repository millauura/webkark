青峰 大輝

Daiki Aomine

About him:

Daiki Aomine (青峰 大輝, Aomine Daiki) was an ace player in the famous Generation of Miracles team, as well as Kuroko's former partner ("light") in Teiko. He is currently a player in the Tōō Academy Basketball Club, serving as the team's Ace, top scorer, and power forward.

He and Kagami are the main rivals in the Interhigh Trials and the Winter Cup. Aomine served as the main antagonist until Seirin's victory in the first round of the Tournament.
